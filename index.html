My Projects

Interlopers in Galaxy Catalogues

My most recent paper on correcting for interlopers in galaxy catalogues for BAO analyses is now on arxiv! We introduced a novel "near/far" shift into a galaxy catalogue to self-calibrate the data for the presence of small displacement (line confusion) interlopers.

LOO-PIT

I'm currently working on applying a cutting edge statistical method called the "Leave One Out-Probability Integral Transform," or LOO-PIT, to cosmological data. This method has a wide variety of potential applications in cosmology. In particular, I am working on determining its sensitivity to the presence of interlopers in galaxy catalogues.
